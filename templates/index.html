Hello MS Band!

This is a simple tool to help band students learn their scales and identify notes for the common major scales.
You can begin by choosing the scale and clef to practice below.
For transposing instruments, a chart is provided below to help you convert your scale to concert pitch.
As more people start practicing, the highest scores will get featured in the leaderboard. Have fun!
